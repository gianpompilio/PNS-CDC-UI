COVID-19 Daily Check-In

Youâ€™re legally required to self-isolate for 14 days or as directed by Public Health if you have tested positive for COVID-19 or have been told by Public Health that you may have been exposed and need to self-isolate.

During your self-isolation, you must register for COVID-19 Daily Check-In and check-in every day until the end of your isolation period.

How to register

You will need the following to complete your registration:

  • Health Card Number
  • iolation end date (as directed by Public Health)
  • email address

What to do after you register

After you register:

  • you will receive email for registration confirmation and daily check-in reminders
  • you must check in every day until the end of your isolation
  • you must continue to self-isolate

Learn more: how to self-isolate.

Privacy Notice

When you use the COVID-19 Daily Check In service your personal information is collected under the authority sections 8, 15 and 50 of the Health Protection Act (PDF). We collect and use this personal information so that we can identify and confirm your restitution within the Justice System as well as process your payment and issue you a receipt.

We use and disclose your personal information for the purposes described above. We may only use or disclose your personal information for another purpose if we are authorized by law to do so, or if we obtain your express consent. To read more about how government respects your privacy when interacting with us review our full Privacy Statement(opens in a new tab). For questions about how your personal information is handled in relation this service, you may contact Service Nova Scotia and Internal Services at askus@novascotia.ca.
