COVID-19 Daily Check-In

You have completed your self-isolation as of January 30, 2021

You are no longer required to complete a daily check-in and no longer required to self-isolate.

What to do next

You may be contacted by Public Health

A Public Health representative may still contact you for questions about your case.

Continue to monitor for symptoms of COVID-19

It is important to continue to monitor your health for symptoms of COVID-19. Complete a COVID-19 Self-assessment if you’re experiencing COVID-19 symptoms. You can call 811 for assessment if you’re unable to complete the COVID-19 Self-assessment online (or to speak with a nurse).

Stay diligent and safe

In addition to getting the vaccine, you need to follow public health directives, including gathering limits, social distancing guidelines, hand washing guidelines and wearing a mask when in public places. You should also stay home if you feel unwell and complete a COVID-19 Self-assessment.
